General

Smartphone Installation

The app can be installed to smartphones homescreens.
This allow the app to run fullscreen without the browser URL bar / menus.

How to add the app to your phone homescreen.

Adaptive Display

The app display change according to the screen size, button labels are removed on small screen, etc.

As much as possible, the version corresponding to the current screen size will be presented in this help page.

Main Menu


The main menu gives you acces to the different parts of the application:

Battle List
the main battle list where you can Add/Edit/Delete/Sort/Export battles.
Statistics
the statistics module to compute and display all your results.
Backup
the page where you can save your battles list to a backup file, load previous backup files,
sync your battles to another device through the web, import your Iron Grudge Data.
Help
Info
legal info.

The number under the title is the app version number (here #test)

Tip : you can force-reload the app by clicking on the title

Bottom Bar


The bottom bar contains page-specific actions (here the battles list page actions).

When no actions are available for a page it is not displayed.
